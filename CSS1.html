Brew the Perfect Cup


A Guide to all time best tea recipes

Everyone has their own tastes and it takes some ability to get the cup of tea right each time. The ingredients need to be proportional, the milk-water ratio should be right, and just enough needs to be brewed to improve the flavors. It's time to experiment until you've perfected the ideal formula and methodology.


Tea Recipes


GREEN TEA WITH MINT HOMEMADE ICED TEA

Ingredients

  • 6 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 Mint Green Tea Bags
  • Juice of 5 orange
  • Ice cubes or crushed ice

Follow the Steps

  • In mug mix cinnamon with a little hot water (approx. 2 Tbsp.) to make a paste. Add vanilla extract.
  • Boil water and allow to cool slightly. Add Lipton Mint Green Tea Bags to mug and brew for 1 1/2 to 2 minutes. Place in your refrigerator to cool.
  • Add orange juice to the brewed tea. Serve in ice filled 12 oz. high ball glasses. Garnish with orange slices.

MEXICAN CHRISTMAS PUNCH

Ingredients

  • 20 whole cloves
  • 2 Tbsp. firmly packed dark brown sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 6 Green Tea Bags
  • 1 cup guava nectar
  • 1 apple, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 cinnamon sticks

Follow the Steps

  • Stick cloves of orange slices onto the flesh. Put in 4 quarts of sugar, orange slices, apples, pears, prunes and cinnamon sticks. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Stir in the sugar. Remove from the heat and add the bags of green tea; cover and steep for five minutes. Stir in the nectar and cut the tea bags.
  • Punch the ladle and the fruit into mugs.
